About

Finance Manager App

A comprehensive financial management application that helps you track budgets, transactions, and receipts.

Features

  • QR Code Receipt Scanning
  • Budget Management
  • Transaction Tracking
  • Category Organization
  • Offline Storage with IndexedDB

Technology Stack

  • Next.js 15 with App Router
  • React 19
  • TypeScript
  • Tailwind CSS
  • Dexie.js for IndexedDB
  • @yudiel/react-qr-scanner

How to Use

  1. Use the Scanner to capture receipt QR codes
  2. Manage your budgets and categories
  3. Track your transactions
  4. View your financial data offline
